Here are the requirements for a degree in table format. I only included upper division major requirements because I have all the other required classes completed. The university offers an MS in CS. Areas of research are broad, including robotics, bioinformatics, and systems engineering.

Term Classes
Semester 1 CS 310 Data Structures, CS 320 Programming Languages, MATH 541 Intro to Numerical Analysis and Computing
Semester 2 CS 370 Computer Architecture, CS 440 Social, Legal and Ethical Issues in Computing, CS 470 UNIX System Administration, CS 503 Scientific Database Techniques
Semester 3 CS 490 Senior Seminar, CS 530 Systems Programming, CS 520 Advanced Programming Languages, CS 524 Compiler Construction.
Semester 4 CS 532 Software Engineering, CS 560 Algorithms and Their Analysis, CS 570 Operating Systems
